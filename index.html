An Iron Will to Overcome Challenges

When I first started, I struggled. I didn't become successful until I got comfortable struggling.

Wake up at 4am, get ready, get dressed, meet with clients to fill out paperwork, do a walk of the floor, reach out to warm clients, do another walk of the floor, reach out to more clients, close up shop, be the last to leave at 8pm. Then when my day ends, I get home and review client paperwork and find a way to get for them the best financing for their vehicle. Rinse and repeat for 60 hours a week. This is my schedule. When I first started, I would go days or weeks without making a single sale, but today I am the frontrunner at my company averaging 2 vehicles a week. The secret to overcoming challenges is to consistently do them everyday, make them comfortable, then add more.
